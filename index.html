About Us

Gujrat's Premier Salt Manufacturer

AI Plus Salts is a leading manufacturer, supplier and exporter of premium-grade salts headquartered in Gujrat, India — the heartland of India's salt industry. From pristine Himalayan pink salt to precision-refined industrial grades, every batch we produce meets rigorous international quality standards.

Our state-of-the-art processing facility handles over 17 distinct salt varieties, serving industries spanning food processing, pharma, chemicals, water treatment, textiles, oil & gas, and beyond.

Quality Assurance

How We Work

From Earth to Industry

Our 5-stage process guarantees consistency, purity and on-time delivery — every single time.

Raw Salt Sourcing
Ethically harvested from pristine Rajasthan salt flats & Himalayan deposits.
02
Precision Processing
