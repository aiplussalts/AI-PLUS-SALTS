Our Philosophy

Salt Deserves Reverence

Most table salt is a shadow of what salt can be — stripped, bleached, and packed with anti-caking agents. We believe in salt the way it has existed for millennia: raw, complex, and full of the minerals the earth placed there. Every grain we sell is harvested by hand, tested for purity, and sourced from producers we've visited ourselves.
